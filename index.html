goodreads

2021

credits

The goodreads platform has undergone a redesign to make it easier to explore new literature, see and create book reviews, and access a vibrant reader community. The brand identity has been updated to appeal to a younger user base, with a focus on a fast-paced platform for sharing ideas and opinions about books. A new added feature is the ability to bookmark and highlight content from physical books using the phones camera.

Which could allow readers to keep track of their favorite passages to share them with others. The new design of the app has been created with simplicity and ease of use in mind. The complex navigation experience has been reduced to a few easy steps and screens.

re:mind

2021

credits

Re:mind is an app that allows users to record chats and gain valuable insights through natural language processing. This technology can structure spoken words in two dimensions based on their semantic proximity, enabling it to reflect on spoken brainstorming sessions, planning, meetings, and more.

The focus is on delivering tangible outcomes from spoken content, such as to-do lists, presentation slides, social media posts, summaries, email drafts, and more. This makes it easy to reflect on past conversations and it's core ideas. Re:mind was born out of an invention design course that encourages to think about new technology and future real-world scenarios.

index insight

2020

credits

Index Insight is an interactive data visualization platform, designed to provide an overview of large index funds. It enables to explore the complex financial landscape through graphical illustrations, highlighting the proportions of various assets within these funds.

Utilizing web scraping techniques enabled the automatic retrieval of index fund data. As a result, it was possible to establish an automated pipeline for the data visualization.
