Goodreads

2021

credits

The Goodreads app has undergone a complete redesign to make it easier for users to explore new literature, see and create book reviews, and access a vibrant reader community. The brand identity has been updated to appeal to a younger user base, with a focus on a fast-paced platform for sharing ideas and opinions about books. A new features is the ability to bookmark and highlight content from physical books using the camera on your phone.

This allows readers to easily keep track of their favorite passages to share them with others. The new design of the app has been created with simplicity and ease of use in mind. The complex navigation experience has been reduced to just a few easy steps and screens. With a focus on community and collaboration, the Goodreads app encourages users to share their thoughts and opinions on the books they've read, creating a dynamic and engaging space for literary discussion.

re:mind

2021

credits

Re:mind is an innovative app that allows users to record chats and gain valuable insights through natural language processing. The app's advanced technology can structure spoken words in two dimensions based on their semantic proximity, enabling it to reflect on spoken brainstorming sessions, planning, meetings, and more. The focus is on delivering tangible outcomes from spoken content, such as to-do lists, presentation slides, social media posts, summaries, email drafts, and more.

This makes it easy to reflect on past conversations and have access to the core ideas, making collaboration and feedback a breeze. Re:mind was born out of an invention design course that incorporates new technology and finds practical applications for it. The concept aims to find a use case for new technology and its potential applications in real-world scenarios.

Index Insight

2020

credits

Index Insight is an interactive data visualization platform, designed to provide an overview of large index funds. It enables users to explore the complex financial landscape through graphical illustrations, highlighting the proportions of various assets within these funds. This approach allows users to easily understand financial data without relying on traditional lists With the inclusion of detailed information about the companies that constitute a fund, Index Insight offers users a unique insight into their investment portfolios.

To make this information more accessible, the platform takes a different approach, specifically for those who may be financially disinterested. By prioritizing user engagement and presenting data in an easily digestible format, Index Insight encourages users to explore the data. The data presented on the platform is obtained using web scraping techniques, enabling index fund data to be fetched automatically and exploring the possibilities of an automated pipeline for data visualization.
