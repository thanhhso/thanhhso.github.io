re:mind
speculative design
2021 spring
The purpose of the speculative design course was to create a future vision that included new technology and find applications for them. Natural Language Processing can be used to structure spoken words in two dimensions based on their semantic proximity. Users can record chats on an app, which is then stored as sharable information. As an outcome, brainstorming sessions are saved, you can always draw on your ideas and get other perspectives on your ideas as you can share them with others.

intersection
interface technologies
2020–2021 winter
Troughout the interface course, we looked at how we could control a vehicle with a game controller. Intersection is a control element that uses the same technical components to maneuver a vehicle in a single motion sequence. By centering on a single motion sequence, the driver may react to curves and different speed situations faster and more precisely. With only two points of touch with the interface, drivers can quickly control their driving. It could be possible to acquire alternative driving postures and forms of mobility.

etf insight
datavisualization
2020–2021 winter
etf insight is an interactive data visualization that graphically illustrates indexes and exchange-traded funds. Its goal is to improve financial data visualization through interactive and visually appealing participation. P5.js and matter.js merges physical attributes with graphical components to dynamically render them. The data was obtained using web scraping techniques and then put into the data visualization. The notion of etf_insight should be able to persuade financially disinterested people to take another approach.

happybriefing
workshopweek project
2020 autumn
With a receipt-like printout and unexpected updates, Happybriefing informs users of their savings. People are so reminded to spend responsibly and to reconsider impulsive purchases. The generated content is intended to relate saving money in a good and humorous way. The thermal printer creates random transactions and therefore indicates how much money was saved.
