Goodreads

2021

credits

The goodreads platform has undergone a complete redesign to make it easier for users to explore new literature, see and create book reviews, and access a vibrant reader community. The brand identity has been updated to appeal to a younger user base, with a focus on a fast-paced platform for sharing ideas and opinions about books. A new feature is the ability to bookmark and highlight content from physical books using the phones camera.

This allows readers to easily keep track of their favorite passages to share them with others. The new design of the app has been created with simplicity and ease of use in mind. The complex navigation experience has been reduced to a few easy steps and screens. With a focus on community and collaboration, the new goodreads encourages users to share their thoughts and opinions on the books they've read, creating a dynamic and engaging space for literary discussion.

Re:mind

2021

credits

Re:mind is an app that allows users to record chats and gain valuable insights through natural language processing. The app's advanced technology can structure spoken words in two dimensions based on their semantic proximity, enabling it to reflect on spoken brainstorming sessions, planning, meetings, and more.

The focus is on delivering tangible outcomes from spoken content, such as to-do lists, presentation slides, social media posts, summaries, email drafts, and more. This makes it easy to reflect on past conversations and have access to the core ideas. Re:mind was born out of an invention design course that encourages to think about new technology and real-world scenarios.

Index Insight

2020

Credits

Index Insight is an interactive data visualization platform, designed to provide an overview of large index funds. It enables to explore the complex financial landscape through graphical illustrations, highlighting the proportions of various assets within these funds. This approach allows to easily understand financial data without relying on traditional lists With the inclusion of detailed information about the companies that constitute a fund,

To make this information more accessible, this concept takes a different approach, specifically for those who may be financially disinterested. By using web scraping techniques, which allowed for the automatic retrieval of index fund data. As a result, it was possible to establish an automated pipeline for the data visualization.
