Goodreads, Application Redesign
Summer 2020

Is it possible to perceive a mutual fund through visuals and interaction?

Individuals can use Goodreads to search a database of books, notes, quotes, and reviews. Users can sign up and register books to create bookshelves and reading lists. They can also form their own groups, along with book recommendations, polls, voting, blogs, and conversations.

Based on previous analysis, it was apparent that we needed to rethink Goodreads present complex navigation structure. The goal was to streamline the complex and complicated navigating experience into a few simple screens.
